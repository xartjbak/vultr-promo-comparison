## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the Vultr new user offer work for existing accounts?**
A: No. Every active promo — the free credit codes, 50% off, and deposit match — explicitly states "new customers only" and "duplicate accounts are not eligible for promotions." If you already have a Vultr account, even an old inactive one, you can't claim a new-user offer on it.

**Q: Can I stack the $300 free credit with the 50% off discount?**
A: No. Vultr's coupon terms state that promotional credit "cannot be combined with any other offers." Pick one offer per account.

**Q: Do I need a credit card to use the free credit codes?**
A: Yes. Vultr requires a valid payment method on file before any promo activates, even for the "free" credit codes. They place a small authorization hold to verify the card. PayPal, Alipay, and cryptocurrency also satisfy this requirement.

**Q: What happens to my instances when the promotional credit runs out?**
A: Once the promo balance hits zero, Vultr starts billing your real payment method at standard hourly rates. If you don't have a valid method on file, instances are suspended. Always destroy instances you no longer need.

**Q: How long does the deposit match bonus last?**
A: 12 months from issuance, according to Vultr's match promo FAQ. The free-credit codes (FLY300VULTR and 250VULTRFLY) expire much faster — 30 days. The 50% off promo ends after your first two invoices.

**Q: Is the Free Tier Program the same as the $300 free credit?**
A: No. They're separate programs. The Free Tier gives one permanently free tiny instance (1 vCPU / 512 MB) to admitted applicants. The $300 free credit is a one-time balance that expires in 30 days and works across any product.
